BookSlice is a Telegram bot that sends you daily paragraphs from a book you want to read.

Getting a paragraph sent to you daily is a great way to (1) build a reading habit and (2) complete a book you're planning to finish.

I got the idea when I found out my friends had religiously read the 100+ book excerpts I had posted on Instagram. Some of them hadn't had time to read a book in years and really enjoyed reading in bite-sizes on Instagram.

I think there are 2 effects happening here: (1) bite-sizes are effective for preventing overwhelm (2) Instagram has a good addictive mechanism built in.
