Simply click the "Start Using PesanBot" button, then add @PesanWalterBot to your Telegram group.

Yes, PesanBot is completely free to use in your Telegram groups.

After compiling orders, upload a photo of the receipt. PesanBot will process the image, match items to orders, and suggest a fair split based on individual orders. It can even handle additional taxes and discounts!
