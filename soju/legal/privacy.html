Privacy Policy for SojuHWP and SojuHWPX

Last updated: 23rd May, 2024

This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is not collected and recorded by SojuHWP and SojuHWPX and how we use it.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

SojuHWP and SojuHWPX are software applications designed solely for the purpose of opening and reading .hwp and .hwpx files respectively. We do not collect any personal data, including but not limited to names, email addresses, or any other type of information that can be used to identify you.

2. No Data Collection

SojuHWP and SojuHWPX do not collect any data from their users. The applications operate entirely offline and do not require an internet connection for their functionality. As such, there is no data transmission to or from the applications.

3. No Third-Party Data Sharing

Since SojuHWP and SojuHWPX do not collect any data, there is no data to share with third parties.

4. Security

The security of your personal information is not applicable since we do not collect any personal information.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
