SojuHWPX is a free application for opening .hwpx files.

Frequently asked questions

.hwpx files are files of "Hangul", a proprietary word processing application published by the South Korean company Hancom Inc. Use of Hangul is the standard when it comes to Korean legal documents, for example see documents from the Financial Services Commission.

There is currently no free / easily available software that can deal with .hwpx or .hwp files. For example, see this issue from LibreOffice. I learnt about this when my friend (who translates and summarizes Korean legal documents as well as documents in other languages) told me about the issue. I also wanted an excuse to try and build a desktop application. In the future I might contribute to the LibreOffice project directly to fix this issue but for now I just wanted to build a simple app that solves this problem.
